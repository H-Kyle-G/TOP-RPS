Current Rules:

  • Each win is +1 to score

  • Each loss is -1 to score, if and only if your current score is greater than 0

  • To exit during a game, just press the cancel button on the prompt

  • Must enter valid choices for each pop-up box:
    • Must enter a whole number greater than 0 for the amount of rounds you want to play
    • Must enter either: Rock, Paper, or Scissor when choosing your move

  • If the player enters an invalid choice, they will be forced to re-enter until it is valid
    • Note that your choice of move is NOT case sensitive (spell it correctly)
    • If you entered an invalid choice of move, you cannot exit the game until a valid choice is entered

  • The computer's choice comes from a pseudo-random function (it is not truly random; no AI)
